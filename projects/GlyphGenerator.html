Glyph Generator

2020 Spring
KTH course DH2642 Interaction Programming and the Dynamic Web Final Project

  • View Glyph Generator Original Site

Glyph Generator is a website using the Spotify API. It generates a visual glyph based on the audio features of the tracks in your playlist, such as danceability, energy, liveness, valence etc. It's not a music player, but a tool to discover the characteristics of your playlists and create a unique cover image for your music.

Contribution

This is a group project cooperated with 3 other KTH students. In this group, I worked as a frontend programmer. I contributed to welcome/glyph/search page, and was responsible for the generation of glyph based on playlists' audio features.
